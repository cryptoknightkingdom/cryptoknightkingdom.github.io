About

CryptoKnightKingdom was created specfically by me, so I am a solo developer in this NFT space. I'm also the Director, Artist, and Marketer for this project, so I apologize if I do not reach out to you immediately for any questions! I have created the Knights you see before you, drawing and exporting over 200 different layers for the Knights.

I am relatively new in the NFT space, and the reason I made this project was not for the sole purpose of money. I created CryptoKnightKingdom because of my love for NFTs and art, and the inspiration for these knights came from my old project idea I made when I was a freshman in highschool. So that just shows another reason why I wanted to make this project for the purpose to revive my dead project once more! Now I know you are probably thinking that this project is just another Pixel Art NFT PFP JPG Project and well... you'd be right! It is similar to how others created their projects, though I hope that does not prevent you from appreciating this project overall!

In the end, I really do want to build a community around this project, and prove that their are souls behind these type of NFT projects as well, and I will do my best to represent that! Thank you again for reading till the end, I appreciate each and every one of you!
